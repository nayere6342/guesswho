Context

For some context, this game was made within two weeks. One day I saw a video of guess who and thought I could try to recreate it, just using what I know about HTML and CSS. I was able to make this, which I ended up testing it out with a couple of family members and it even worked out at the end. I had people suggest that I add a scoring system to the game. Which I didn't even think about adding one until someone asked for one.
