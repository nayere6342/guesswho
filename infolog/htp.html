How To Play

Identifier

Once a player has choose a category. The player is given a random card of the identifier. Then, without showing to the other player ask questions of what their identifier might be.

Hints

Hints are a one time tool. A player is only allowed to show a hint when requested, it shows details like this identifier traits or time periods.

Points

Points help to know the score between players. Add or remove scoreboards based on how many players their are. Depending on who has the most points, it will display a crown over the player with the most points.
